I'm a Software Engineer and researcher passionate about developing innovative and meaningful technology solutions. Currently, I work as a scientific assistant in the KITE II research project, where we focus on creating AI-driven applications that support female entrepreneurs in building resilience against discriminatory experiences during the startup process. This involves using cutting-edge techniques like bias detection in AI algorithms to ensure fair and empowering solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, and during my studies, I specialized in Games Engineering. My thesis, titled "Noise-based Dynamic Terrain Generation with Unity Compute Shaders", explored procedural terrain generation using advanced techniques like Compute Shaders, Fractal Brownian Motion (FBM), and Domain Warping.

Outside of my academic and professional work, I enjoy engaging in creative pursuits like crocheting, knitting, and other handicrafts, which provide a refreshing balance to my tech-driven projects.

Through my work, I strive to bridge the gap between innovative technology and practical solutions, contributing to projects that promote inclusivity, creativity, and positive social impact.
